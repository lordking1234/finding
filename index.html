What is Fundraising

When a business reaches a certain stage of development, it looks to fundraising in order to secure money and fund the next stage of development. This happens when companies need more capital to scale quickly compared with what’s available to them at present. Crypto businesses are not immune from this but have more options at their disposal. So, if a crypto startup is planning to secure capital, it needs to look into raising funds. If the startup is either too early or too late in the developmental stage, however, it might not qualify for any of the traditional ways of fundraising like initial public offerings and securing a bank loan.

The crypto and blockchain space is presenting novel approaches to raising funds that have not been possible in the past.
